about us 

Looking for a club where you can bring your tinkering dreams to life? Look no further, Makerspace is the one for you. We make anything and everything, from hardware to software, from small-scale to big- scale. In Makerspace, you will find fellow passionate makers who are just as excited to create things just as you are. You will be able to create anything you can dream of using FabLab resources and working with like-minded friends. As long as you want to make something, or have any ideas on making something, we warmly welcome you to join us on Wednesday afternoon, from 3-5pm!
